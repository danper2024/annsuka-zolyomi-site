Annuska Zolyomi

Annuska Zolyomi is an Assistant Professor at the University of Washington Bothell, specializing in user experience research, inclusive design, and accessibility. With a background in industry and academia, she brings a human-centered lens to technology development and pedagogy. Her work explores how design can reflect the emotional, sensory, and social experiences of diverse communities, especially autistic adults.
