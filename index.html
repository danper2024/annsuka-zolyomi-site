Annuska Zolyomi

I am an educator, user experience researcher and designer, and accessibility specialist.

Experience Skillset Goals Highlights

My Experience

I am an Assistant Professor at the University of Washington Bothell. I hold a PhD in Information Science and a Master’s in Human-Centered Design and Engineering. My industry experience includes roles as a program manager, product planner, and accessibility strategist.

I’ve consulted for technology user experience projects since 2013, working with clients such as Microsoft, Amazon, Easter Seals, and the Joan Ganz Cooney Center at Sesame Workshop.

My Skillset

I specialize in inclusive human-computer interaction (HCI), leading research and design initiatives to create intuitive and innovative technology products. My methods include participatory design and grounded research approaches.

My doctoral work focused on communication and affective computing that reflects the social, emotional, and sensory experiences of autistic adults.

My Goals

I aim to bring an inclusive lens to HCI and software engineering pedagogy. Through scholarship and collaboration, I work with diverse, creative teams to advance knowledge, education, and technology for social good.
