Inclusive Tech Lab

Lab Purpose

This space supports collaborative research, design, and advocacy. We work with community partners to prototype accessible tools and challenge dominant narratives in tech.

  • Current members: UX designers, disability activists, HCI researchers
  • Projects: Accessible journaling apps, sensory-friendly web design
  • Open to new collaborators and student researchers
