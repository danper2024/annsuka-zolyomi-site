Research

Annuska Zolyomi’s research explores inclusive design, disability studies, and human-computer interaction. Her work centers on co-designing technologies with marginalized communities and challenging ableist assumptions in tech development.

Research Themes

  • Disability justice and technology
  • Participatory design with neurodivergent users
  • Critical approaches to accessibility

Selected Publications

  • "Designing with Autistic Adults: Participatory Methods for Inclusive Tech" – CHI 2023
  • "Reframing Accessibility: A Justice-Oriented Lens" – ASSETS 2022
  • "Sensory-Friendly Interfaces: Co-Designing with Neurodivergent Youth" – IDC 2021
