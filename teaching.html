Teaching

Annuska Zolyomi teaches courses on inclusive design, disability studies, and human-centered computing. Her pedagogy emphasizes critical reflection, participatory methods, and community engagement.

Courses Taught

  • INFO 360: Design Thinking
  • INFO 474: Inclusive Tech & Accessibility
  • INFO 200: Intellectual Foundations of Informatics

Teaching Philosophy

Annuska fosters a classroom environment that values lived experience, challenges dominant narratives, and encourages students to co-create knowledge. She integrates universal design principles and invites students to reflect on the social impact of technology.
